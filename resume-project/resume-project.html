About

Madison Peek is a junior and a reporter for the Howard Center from Poolesville, Maryland. She is studying journalism and criminology and criminal justice at the University of Maryland. She’s previously worked as a campus life writer, a reporter, a copy editor and as a general assignment editor for a variety of news outlets.

Although she was a force to be reckoned with in her elementary school newspaper, Madison began truly pursuing her journalism career during college to learn more about the world and make it a better place. She’s always wanted to follow in the footsteps of investigative journalists like Nellie Bly and Ida B. Wells who learned about pervasive issues in society and then brought light to them.
